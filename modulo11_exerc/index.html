About the bookstore

Founded in 2009, Freak Reader has always been more than just a place to buy books. Nestled in the heart of Manhatthan, we're a sanctuary for readers, thinkers, and dreamers. Our shelves are filled with stories that have the power to transport, enlighten, and inspire.

From the classics that have shaped generations to contemporary works that are redefining the literary landscape, our curated selection reflects our passion for the written word and our commitment to serving the reading community.
